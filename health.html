Pea&Spinach Carbonara

Nutrition Facts (per serving): 430 Cal & 15g Fat & 54g Carbs & 20g Protein

Ingredients:

  • 1 ½ tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • ½ cup panko breadcrumbs, preferably whole-wheat
  • 1 small clove garlic, minced
  • 8 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese, divided
  • 3 tablespoons finely chopped fresh parsley
  • 3 large egg yolks
  • 1 large egg
  • ½ teaspoon ground pepper
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • 1 (9 ounce) package fresh tagliatelle or linguine
  • 8 cups baby spinach
  • 1 cup peas (fresh or frozen)

Instructions:

  1. Put 10 cups of water in a large pot and bring to a boil over high heat.
  2. heat oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add breadcrumbs and garlic; cook, stirring frequently, until toasted, about 2 minutes.
  3. Whisk the remaining 6 tablespoons Parmesan, egg yolks, egg, pepper and salt in a medium bow.
  4. Cook pasta in the boiling water, stirring occasionally, for 1 minute.
  5. Add spinach and peas and cook until the pasta is tender, about 1 minute more.
  6. Reserve 1/4 cup of the cooking water. Drain and place in a large bowl.
  7. Slowly whisk the reserved cooking water into the egg mixture.
  8. Gradually add the mixture to the pasta, tossing with tongs to combine.
  9. Serve topped with the reserved breadcrumb mixture.

Spinach&Feta Scrambled Egg Pitas

Nutrition Facts (per serving): 303 Cal & 16g Fat & 21g Carbs & 20g Protein

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 (10 ounce) block frozen chopped spinach, thawed, drained and squeezed dry
  • Pinch salt
  • 8 large eggs, beaten
  • ¼ cup finely crumbled feta cheese
  • Freshly ground pepper to taste
  • 8 teaspoons sun-dried tomato tapenade or sun-dried tomato pesto
  • 4 whole-wheat pitas (5-inch), cut in half, warmed if desired (see Tip)

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil in a large nonstick skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add spinach and salt and cook until steaming hot, stirring occasionally.
  3. Add eggs and cook, stirring the eggs as they set, until they form soft curds and are just moist, 4 to 5 minutes.
  4. Add feta and pepper and cook until set.
  5. Spread tapenade (or pesto) inside pita pockets, 2 teaspoons per pita.
  6. Divide the egg mixture among the pitas.
  7. Tip: To warm pita: Wrap in foil; bake in toaster oven at 350 degrees F until hot, 8 to 10 minutes.

Refried Bean&Pico de Gallo Tostadas

Nutrition Facts (per serving): 276 Cal & 6g Fat & 50g Carbs & 7g Protein

Ingredients:

  • 8 corn tortillas
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • ¼ teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1 (15 ounce) can refried beans, warmed
  • 2 cups pico de gallo

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F.
  2. Arrange tortillas on a large baking sheet.
  3. Use a pastry brush to brush both sides of each tortilla lightly with oil.
  4. Sprinkle with salt.
  5. Bake for 5 minutes, flip and cook until lightly browned, about 5 more minutes.
  6. Spread warmed refried beans evenly over the tortillas and top each with pico de gallo.

Farro Bowl with Rotisserie Chicken

Nutrition Facts (per serving): 288 Cal & 3g Fat & 40g Carbs & 27g Protein

Ingredients:

  • 1 (8.5 ounce) package microwaveable farro
  • 1 (10 ounce) Mediterranean-style salad kit
  • 1 cup chopped rotisserie chicken

Instructions:

  1. Microwave farro according to package directions; set aside to cool slightly.
  2. Toss together salad kit in a large bowl. Add chicken and the cooled farro; toss to combine.
