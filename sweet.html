Peaches and Cream Puff Pastry Tart

Ingredients:

  • 1 sheet of puff pastry
  • 8 oz dairy-free cream cheese
  • 1-2 tbsp agave nectar or sweetener
  • 1.5 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1-2 tsp vegan butter
  • 2 cups peaches
  • 0.5 cup blueberries

Instructions:

  1. Thaw puff pastry and soften cream cheese.
  2. Preheat oven to 400ºF and line a baking sheet.
  3. Beat cream cheese with agave nectar and vanilla extract.
  4. Roll out pastry, brush with vegan butter, and sprinkle sugar.
  5. Spread cream cheese on pastry.
  6. Top with peaches and blueberries.
  7. Bake at 400ºF for 25-30 minutes.
  8. Cut into squares and enjoy!

Basque Cheesecake

Ingredients:

  • 1 ¼ cups ground vanilla wafer cookies or graham crackers
  • ¼ tsp kosher salt
  • 6 Tbsp melted unsalted butter
  • 16 oz cream cheese (room temp)
  • ½ cup granulated sugar
  • 3 large eggs (room temp)
  • 1 ½ tsp pure vanilla extract
  • 2 Tbsp all-purpose flour
  • ½ tsp kosher salt
  • 1 cup heavy cream

Instructions:

  1. Line a 6-inch round cake pan with parchment paper.
  2. Preheat oven to 425°F.
  3. Make the crust: Mix cookies, salt, and melted butter. Press into the pan.
  4. Make the filling: Blend cream cheese, sugar, and vanilla. Add eggs one by one.
  5. Mix flour, salt, and cream. Combine with the cream cheese mixture.
  6. Pour the mixture onto the crust and bake for 35-40 minutes at 425°F.
  7. Cool to room temperature and serve.

French Pear Cake

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter (room temperature)
  • 1 cup white granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs (room temperature)
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 1 1/3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/4 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp fine salt
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 3 pears (ripe, peeled, and cored)
  • 1/4 cup white granulated sugar (for topping)
  • 1/3 cup flaked almonds (optional, for topping)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F and prepare a 10-inch springform pan.
  2. Prepare the pears by slicing and arranging them in the pan.
  3. In a large bowl, beat butter and sugar until fluffy.
  4. Add eggs one at a time, then add vanilla.
  5. In a separate bowl, whisk flour, baking powder, and salt. Add to the butter mixture alternately with sour cream.
  6. Spoon the batter into the pan and spread it thinly. Top with almonds and sugar.
  7. Bake for 35-40 minutes until a tester comes out clean.
  8. Let the cake cool, remove the ring, and enjoy with Crème Fraîche.

Peanut Butter Chocolate Oat Cups

Ingredients:

  • 70g rolled oats (gluten-free if necessary)
  • 2 tbsp smooth peanut butter
  • 2 tbsp maple syrup
  • Pinch of salt
  • 100g smooth peanut butter (for the middle layer)
  • 1 tbsp maple syrup (for the middle layer)
  • 70g dark chocolate (melted, for the topping)
  • Optional: melted Biscoff spread for drizzling

Instructions:

  1. Mix rolled oats, peanut butter, maple syrup, and a pinch of salt in a bowl. Press into a 6-cup silicon muffin tray and freeze for 15 minutes.
  2. In a small bowl, mix peanut butter and maple syrup to create the middle layer. Spread it over the oat bases.
  3. Top with melted dark chocolate.
  4. Refrigerate until hardened. Enjoy!
