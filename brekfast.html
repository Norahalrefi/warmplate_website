Banana Overnight Oats

Ingredients:

Ingredients:
  • 1 cup cashew milk
  • 1/2 cup gluten-free rolled oats
  • 1/4 cup chopped or shredded zucchini
  • 1-2 scoops protein powder
  • 1 tablespoon chia seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1 banana, halved lengthwise
  • 1 teaspoon coconut oil
  • Blueberries
  • Almond butter
  • Vegan yogurt
  • Granola
Instructions:
  1. Combine milk, oats, zucchini, protein powder, chia seeds, cinnamon, and salt in a jar. Refrigerate overnight. Eat cold or heat it up in the morning!
  2. When ready to serve, heat the coconut oil in a small skillet. Place the banana, cut-side down, on the pan and cook for a few minutes, until golden. Flip and cook 1-2 minutes, then place on top of the oatmeal.
  3. Top with blueberries, almond butter, yogurt, and granola.

Strawberry Crepes

Ingredients:
  • 1/4 cup protein powder
  • 1 cup sparkling water (divided)
  • 1 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon granulated sugar
  • 1 cup almond milk
  • 2 tablespoons melted coconut oil
  • Strawberries for serving
  • Vanilla coconut yogurt for serving
Instructions:
    Combine protein powder and 1/4 cup sparkling water in a small bowl. In a large bowl, whisk together flour, cornstarch, and sugar. Add protein powder mixture, remaining sparkling water, almond milk, and melted coconut oil. Whisk until smooth. Heat coconut oil in a non-stick pan over medium heat. Pour 1/3 cup of batter into the pan and spread it into a thin circle. Cook until set in the middle, about 2 minutes. Flip and cook for 30 seconds. Repeat with the rest of the batter. Serve with strawberries and cream, if desired.

Melted Caprese Sandwich

Ingredients:
  • 1 ciabatta loaf
  • 1 cup grated Mozzarella cheese
  • 2 tomatoes, sliced
  • 1 ball buffalo mozzarella / fior di latte
  • 2 tbsp basil pesto
Instructions:
  1. Preheat the oven's grill.
  2. Split the ciabatta in half and drizzle with olive oil. Toast under the grill until golden brown.
  3. Spread the pesto onto the bottom half of the bread and top with grated mozzarella and tomatoes. Season the tomatoes, then place under the grill again until the cheese is melted.
  4. Remove from the oven, then top with the fresh mozzarella, basil leaves (optional), and more pesto.
  5. Top with the other half of the bread, slice, and serve.

Shakshuka

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 2 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • ¼ tsp chili powder
  • 1 can whole peeled tomatoes
  • 6 large eggs
  • Salt and pepper
  • Fresh cilantro and parsley

Instructions:

  1. Heat olive oil in a pan, sauté onion and bell pepper.
  2. Add garlic and spices, cook briefly.
  3. Pour tomatoes, season, and simmer.
  4. Create wells in the sauce, crack eggs, cover, and cook.
  5. Garnish with cilantro and parsley.
