Mini Apple Hand Pies

Ingredients:

  • 2 Apples (fuji or honeycrisp)
  • Butter
  • Brown Sugar
  • Cinnamon
  • Lemon Juice
  • Cornstarch

Instructions:

  1. Everything is combined in a saucepan, over medium heat
  2. After about 10 minutes, the apples will tenderize and release their juices.
  3. The mixture will seem a bit runny, which is why the cornstarch comes in.
  4. This immediately thickens up the filling to give it a perfect jammy texture.

Fluffy pittas

Ingredients:

  • 500g strong white bread flour, plus extra for dusting
  • 7g sachet fast-action dried yeast
  • 1 tbsp caster sugar
  • 2 tsp fresh thyme leaves (optional
  • 200ml milk
  • 1 tbsp olive oil, plus a little extra for proving and brushing​

Instructions:

  1. Mix the flour, yeast, sugar, thyme, if using, and 2 tsp salt in a bowl with your fingertips.
  2. Pour over the milk, olive oil and 100ml lukewarm water.
  3. Tip onto a work surface and knead for 8-10 mins.
  4. Lightly oil a bowl, put the dough in the bowl and cover with a tea towel.
  5. Leave to rise for 1 hr or until nearly doubled in size.
  6. Divide the dough into eight equal pieces. One at a time, on a lightly floured surface, roll out to circles about 20cm wide.
  7. heat a frying pan or over a medium-high heat
  8. cook, one by one in each pan, until bubbles appear on the surface after 1-2 mins.
  9. When the underside is golden, flip and cook for another 2-3 mins.

Focaccia

Ingredients:

  • 500g strong bread flour, plus extra for dusting
  • 7g dried fast action yeast
  • 2 tsp fine sea salt
  • 5 tbsp olive oil, plus extra for the tin and to serve
  • 1 tsp flaky sea salt
  • ¼ small bunch of rosemary, sprigs pickedl

Instructions:

  1. Sift flour into a large mixing bowl. Mix the yeast on one side, and the fine salt on the other side. Then mix them together.
  2. Make a well in the middle and add 2 tablespoons of oil and 350-400 ml of lukewarm water.
  3. Sprinkle the work surface with flour, then spread the dough on it.
  4. Knead for 5-10 minutes until the dough becomes smooth and less sticky.
  5. Place the dough in a clean bowl, then cover it and leave it to ferment for an hour.
  6. Grease a flat rectangular mold with oil. Place the dough on the work surface, then spread it out. Cover it and leave it for 35-45 minutes.
  7. Preheat the oven to 220°C.
  8. Mix 1 ½ tablespoons olive oil, 1 tablespoon water and salt flakes, then sprinkle them over the bread.
  9. Bake for 20 mins until golden. Whilst the bread is still hot, drizzle over 1-2 tbsp olive oil.

Croissants

Ingredients:

  • 500g strong white flour, plus extra for dusting
  • 1 ½ tsp salt
  • 50g sugar
  • 2 x 7g sachets fast-action dried yeast
  • oil, for greasing
  • 300g butter, at room temperature
  • 1 milk

Instructions:

  1. make easy dough from butter, flour, sugar, salt, yeast, and milk.
  2. roll out dough into a large rectangle.
  3. make the butter layer.
  4. enclose the butter layer inside the dough.
  5. roll out the dough into another large rectangle, then fold it back together.
  6. roll out the dough again, fold it back together again.
  7. roll out the dough one more time, fold it back together.
  8. shape the croissants.
  9. bake.
