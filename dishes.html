Tomato Ricotta Pasta

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Cherry tomatoes
  • 3 Garlic cloves
  • 8 oz Mushrooms
  • 1/2 Red onion
  • 1/4 cup Tomato paste
  • 8 oz Rotini pasta, dry
  • 1 tbsp Butter
  • 1 cup Mozzarella cheese
  • 2 tsp Salt
  • 2 cups Water

Instructions:

  1. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat
  2. Add sliced garlic and diced red onion, sauté until fragrant and onions are translucent
  3. Stir in cherry tomatoes, mushrooms, and green olives. Cook until mushrooms are tender
  4. Add tomato paste, Italian seasoning, black pepper, and salt. Stir to combine
  5. Meanwhile, cook the rotini pasta according to package instructions in a separate pot
  6. Drain the pasta and add it to the tomato and mushroom mixture
  7. Stir in mozzarella cheese until it melts and the pasta is coated with the sauce
  8. Serve hot, garnished with fresh herbs if desired

Cajun Shrimp and Rice

Ingredients:

  • 1 ½ teaspoons paprika
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • ½ teaspoon cracked black pepper
  • ½ teaspoon onion powder
  • ½ teaspoon dried oregano - may sub an Italian herb blend or Herbs de Provence
  • ½ teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • ¼ teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes

Instructions:

  1. Whisk together all of the ingredients for the Cajun seasoning.
  2. Melt 2 tablespoons butter in a large skillet over medium heat. Stir in garlic, half of the Cajun seasoning, and the rice.
  3. Stir in chicken broth, bring to a boil, reduce to a simmer, and cover. Cook for 15 minutes, stirring 1-2 times throughout.
  4. While rice is cooking, prepare the shrimp by stirring together the remaining 2 tablespoons melted butter and the remaining Cajun seasoning. Pour over shrimp and toss to coat.
  5. Stir shrimp into the rice, cover, and cook 3-5 minutes longer until shrimp is pink and opaque.
  6. Garnish with chopped parsley if desired and serve.

GARLIC AND ROSEMARY BEEF TIPS

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 lbs Beef tips
  • 2 tbsp Garlic
  • 1 tbsp Garlic powder
  • 1 Onion
  • 1 sprig Rosemary
  • 1 sprig Thyme, fresh
  • 2 cups Beef broth
  • 1/4 cup Worcestershire sauce
  • 1/3 cup Flour
  • Salt and pepper
  • 3 tbsp Olive oil
  • 1 tbsp Onion powder

Instructions:

  1. Heat 3 tablespoons of olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Toss beef tips in flour, salt, and pepper. Add them to the skillet and brown on all sides. Remove from the skillet and set aside.
  3. In the same skillet, add minced garlic, onion, and fresh thyme. Sauté until the onion is translucent.
  4. Sprinkle in garlic powder and onion powder. Stir well.
  5. Pour in beef broth and Worcestershire sauce. Stir and bring to a simmer.
  6. Return the browned beef tips to the skillet and add the rosemary sprig.
  7. Cover the skillet and let it simmer for about 20-25 minutes until the beef is tender.
  8. Remove the rosemary sprig and thyme sprig before serving.

GARLIC BUTTER CHICKEN

Ingredients:

  • 1 tsp 21 salute seasoning or poultry seasoning
  • 1 1/2 lbs Chicken breasts, boneless skinless
  • 1 tbsp Garlic
  • 2 tsp Parsley, fresh
  • 1/2 cup Chicken broth
  • 1 tsp Black pepper, freshly ground
  • 1 tsp Kosher salt
  • 3 tbsp Olive oil
  • 3 tbsp Butter, unsalted

Instructions:

  1. Sprinkle the 21 salute seasoning (or poultry seasoning), kosher salt, and freshly ground black pepper evenly over the chicken breasts.
  2. Heat 3 tablespoons of olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  3. Add the seasoned chicken breasts to the skillet and cook for about 6-7 minutes on each side or until cooked through and no longer pink in the center.
  4. Remove the cooked chicken from the skillet and set it aside.
  5. In the same skillet, add 3 tablespoons of unsalted butter and minced garlic. Sauté for about a minute until fragrant.
  6. Pour in the chicken broth and bring it to a simmer, stirring to combine with the pan drippings.
  7. Return the cooked chicken to the skillet and let it simmer for a few more minutes, allowing the sauce to thicken and coat the chicken.
  8. Garnish with fresh parsley and serve.
